Featured Project

CampusFix

Campus Technology

Smart Campus Issue Resolution System

CampusFix is a student-built web platform designed to bridge the gap between students and college administration at Lachoo Memorial College. Students can report infrastructure issues, maintenance problems, and campus concerns through a simple, intuitive interface — and track resolution progress in real time.

Report Issues Instantly

Submit campus problems — broken equipment, infrastructure, safety — with photos and location details.

Track Resolution Progress

Real-time status updates as administration acknowledges and resolves reported issues.

Community Voting

Students can upvote issues to prioritize high-impact problems, ensuring the most critical ones are solved first.

Admin Dashboard

A dedicated admin panel for college management to view, assign, and close reported issues efficiently.
